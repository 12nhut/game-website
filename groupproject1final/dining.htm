The Cheesecake Factory

The Cheesecake Factory in Waikiki is the only Cheesecake Factory on Oahu! It is located across the international marketplace and is steps away from Waikiki beach!

Red Lobster

Red Lobster is located at the beginning of Waikiki and is in walking distance from Ala Moana Shopping Center! It is a few steps from the beach and right next to a beautiful pier.
