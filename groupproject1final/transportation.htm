Oahu's city tranportation is named "TheBus". Fares are reasonable and special passes for visitors also offered. For more information, please visit TheBus's home page at http://www.thebus.org.
The bus routes below are buses that go to and from the popular Waikiki area.









Trolley

Although the trolley's routes are not extensive as TheBus, but the trolley visits most of the visitors' attractions. They also offers tickets for unlimited rides over a certain number of days. For more information about the trolley, please visit http://www.waikikitrolley.com

Taxi (TheCab)

Taxis are another great way to travel when in a group and the cost can be shared. You can find taxi stands at hotels, most major shopping malls and at the airport. Note that using taxi stands and calling for pick-ups by phone or via hotel concierge is the norm in Hawaii rather than hailing a cab.
