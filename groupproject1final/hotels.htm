Hotels

The Royal Hawaiian

The Royal Hawaiian, also known as the Pink Palace of the Pacific, was one of the first hotels established in Waikiki during 1927. As soon as their doors opened, a non-stop flood of tourists from the mainland United States poured through its doors. It served and still serves as the Pacific home to the world's most influential statesmen and Hollywood stars. To book a room or to learn more, visit their official website: Royal Hawaiian.




Turtle Bay Resort

The Turtle Bay Resort is the only luxury beach resort on the North Shore of Oahu. Their oceanfront setting immerses guests in the natural tranquility of the North Shore lifestyle. Oahu's spectacular ocean views are on display from every room and the resort also includes beach cottages and villas. They also offer a variety of actitives such as horseback riding, segway tours, tennis, etc. The Turtle Bay Resort is the only resort on Oahu that offers onsite helicopter tours. To book a room or to learn more, visit their official website: Turtle Bay Resort.


Kahala Mandarin

The only beachfront resort is located in Oahu’s most prestigious residential area, Kahala. Overlooking both Diamond Head and Koko Head craters, it is a great location for special events or weddings. The Kahala Mandarin is known for the sheer opulence of its rooms and suites and for the innovation of its restaurants. To book a room or to learn more, visit their official website: Kahala Manadarin.




Trump International Hotel Waikiki

This Hawaii luxury hotel is part of the Waikiki Beach Walk development offering vibrant entertainment, exclusive boutiques and world-class restaurants, making it one of the premier Honolulu hotels. It is also one of the newer hotels located in Waikiki. To book a room or to learn more, visit their official website: Trump International Hotel Waikiki.


Halekulani

The Halekulani (meaning "House Befitting Heaven") Hotel was one of the first hotels located in the Waikiki area back in the 1920's. It is known for its rooms, suites, and restaurants. A unique feature of the hotel is the Halekulani Suite which has an interior designed by famous designer, Vera Wang. They are also known for their great tasting buffet at Orchids and their wedding accommodations. To book a room or to learn more, visit their official website: Halekulani.
